About Me

Hello! I'm Joseph Junior Mensah, a passionate Computer Engineering student exploring the exciting world of technology. My interests lie in both hardware and software, and I'm always looking to push the boundaries of what I can create.

Currently studying at Cornell University, my focus is on computer architecture, programming languages, and building efficient systems that can drive technological innovation. I'm enthusiastic about collaborating with like-minded people to work on impactful projects that make a difference.

More About My Journey

I started my journey with a fascination for how things work under the hood, from the circuitry inside devices to the code running them. This curiosity has driven me to pursue a career where I can contribute to advancing both hardware and software technologies.

In my free time, I enjoy diving into audio engineering, performing circus arts, and solving complex problems. These hobbies have helped me develop a unique perspective on technology and how it interacts with other creative fields.
